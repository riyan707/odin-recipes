Tabbouleh

Description

Tabbouleh is basically parsley salad. There’s technically no lettuce in it. It’s a salad made up of parsley, tomatoes, bulgur wheat, and green onions – all finely chopped and tossed with olive oil and lemon juice!

Tabbouleh salad is also probably the most popular Mediterranean salad!

Ingredients

  • Parsley
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Onions
  • Bulgur
  • Lemon Juice (freshly squeezed)
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Begin by whisking olive oil and lemon juice in a bowl, this will be the salad dressing
  2. Add the bulgur to the salad dressing and allow to sit on the side
  3. Start by chopping up the vegetables in any order (ensure that all is chopped into small fine pieces)
  4. Put chopped vegetables into a bowl
  5. Transfer the salad dressing and bulgur onto the chopped vegetables
  6. Gently mix everything together
  7. ENJOY!
